Decision trees - simulating real behavior

Game goal

The main goal is to eliminate the enemy and reach the winning score - number of kills.
Players have one objective - search and destroy. While exploring the map, they find the powerups (description below) which will boost their stats and help them get the enemy faster and easier.

Each player is able to fight with the enemy with his fists, inflicting damage (5 health points). The attack is possible while standing on the tile next to the enemy.

When the player health drops below critical threshold, he flees if he is able to. If not, attacks as a last resort of defence.

Bonuses

Bonuses help the player in these ways:

  • Health bonus - instant +20 of players health.
  • Speed bonus - +1 action per frame step, 10 frame steps timeout.
  • Damage bonus - +2 damage points, permanent (till death).
  • Armor bonus - +5 armor points.
Coding challenge

Main goal is to implement the Decision tree to simulate behavior of the game player.
